About Us

Here at A Nurturing Touch, we touch your loved one's life with love and care. Located in the beautiful residential neighborhood in the city of Oak Park. Our mission is to provide compassionate, personalized care that enriches the lives of our residents. We are dedicated to fostering a warm, supportive environment where each individual is respected, valued, and encouraged to maintain their independence. Our skilled team is committed to delivering high-quality care, ensuring comfort, safety, and dignity while promoting a vibrant and fulfilling lifestyle for all our residents.

Services

Personal Care

  • Assistance with Daily Activities: Help with activities of daily living (ADLs) such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting.
  • Mobility Assistance: Support with moving around the facility, whether using walkers, wheelchairs, or other mobility aids.

Healthcare Services

  • Medication Management: Administration and monitoring of medications, including prescription refills and coordination with healthcare providers.
  • Medical Care: Access to on-site or visiting medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, and specialists for routine check-ups and treatment.

Nutritional Services

  • Meal Planning and Preparation: Nutritious, balanced meals prepared on-site, accommodating special dietary needs and preferences.
  • Hydration: Regular monitoring to ensure residents stay hydrated.

Housekeeping and Maintenance

  • Cleaning Services: Regular cleaning and maintenance of residents’ rooms and common areas to ensure a sanitary environment.
  • Laundry Services: Washing, drying, and folding of personal clothing and linens.

Safety and Security

  • 24/7 Supervision: Staff available around the clock to provide assistance and respond to emergencies.
  • Emergency Response Systems: Installation of call systems or alarms for immediate assistance if needed.

Transportation Services

  • Scheduled Transportation: Arrangements for medical appointments, shopping, or recreational outings.
  • Transportation Assistance: Help with getting to and from appointments or events.

Administrative Services

  • Personalized Care Plans: Development of individualized care plans tailored to each resident’s needs and preferences.
  • Family Communication: Regular updates and communication with family members about the resident’s care and well-being.

End-of-Life Care

  • Palliative and Hospice Care: Supportive care for residents with terminal conditions, focusing on comfort, pain management, and emotional support.
